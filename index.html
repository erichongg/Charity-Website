St. Joseph Mission Charity

“It’s not how much we give but how much love we put into giving.” — Saint Teresa of Calcutta

St. Joseph Mission Charity was founded by a dedicated group of Catholic friends who are deeply committed to living out the principles of Catholic Social Teaching, which emphasizes human dignity, solidarity, and the preferential option for the poor.

Guided by our faith, we strive to provide compassionate care, resources, and support to those most in need, particularly the impoverished and marginalized communities around the world. Our mission extends globally, with a special focus on assisting individuals and families affected by leprosy.

Through the generosity of our donors, volunteers, and faith-based partners, we continue to expand our outreach efforts, ensuring that no one is forgotten or left behind. Whether through medical missions, community development projects, or direct financial assistance, our goal is to be a beacon of light and hope for those suffering the most.
